About Mentormind

We guide less experienced people professionally in their careers and personal lives. We usually make it easier for our mentees to realize their professional path in the shortest possible time by providing advice and support, as well as answering their questions. In other words, we participate in the role of a person who will support the mentors to share their experiences, ways of using the available resources correctly, and to make this period more useful.

Career mentor

These individuals are in a more complete position than their mentees. They are more informed persons in the field of experience, knowledge and mainly act as a career guide. Career mentors help their mentees develop where and what steps they can take based on their readiness potential.

personal development mentor.

A personal development mentor can mainly guide you in your personal development. They are helpful in guiding you through difficult decisions in your career development. For example, they may suggest to their mentees that they need to leave their current workplace or change their career in another direction. And we do not hesitate to provide you with perfect psychological support, we offer you psychologists in different fields. Each of us needs a psychologist in life, and most importantly, we will support teenagers in their difficult programs and life paths. We are always ready to help you!
